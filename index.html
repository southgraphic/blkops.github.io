We are BLKOPS

Our goal is to create tools that make it easier to handle the logistics and housekeeping of grass roots community involvement and activism.

An effective movement requires coordination and organization. As concerned citizens with common interests we are here to support community organizers, non-violent protesters, and those mobilizing efforts to unravel systemic racism and racial oppression.
